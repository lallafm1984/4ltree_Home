## 전체 설명

```text
Wink Timer is a timer and stopwatch app for everyday timing.

Use it as a normal button-based timer first. Start, pause, resume, reset, set timer alerts, and record stopwatch laps when you need a quick way to measure time.

When you want more flexible control, switch to assisted modes that let you use your eyes, face, or device movement to control timing.

Key features

- Basic Timer: start, pause, resume, and reset with buttons
- Stopwatch: measure elapsed time and record laps
- Timer alerts: use sound and vibration settings
- LOOK PAUSE: pause and resume based on whether you are looking at the screen
- WINK CONTROL: use wink gestures for timer actions and lap records
- FLIP TIMER: flip your device face down to start, then face up to pause
- SMILE MODE: use smile detection for timer control
- History: review recent timing records and timeline events

Wink Timer is not limited to focus or study sessions. Use it as a cooking timer, workout timer, study timer, work timer, break timer, or quick stopwatch for everyday routines.

The basic timer and stopwatch are simple to use, while camera and sensor-assisted modes add a more hands-free way to manage time when touching the screen is inconvenient.

Camera-based features may vary by device performance, lighting, face angle, and camera position.
```
